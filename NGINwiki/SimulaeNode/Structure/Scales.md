The [[Scales]] are collections of organized sets of related lookups typically indicating various qualities of the given [[SimulaeNode]]

The Political and Personality scales are used by the [[Socialization]] system to provide nuanced and realistic differences between [[Simulae Actor]]s for the purposes of interesting social-interactions between sentient entities.

The existing scales are for:
- [[Politics Scale]]
- [[Personality Scale]]

> ex: Scales structure
```json
Scales = {
	'Personality': {...},
	'Policy': {...},
	...
}
```
