[[Faction Warfare]] is a [[Gamemode]] where factions compete for territory, resources, and strategic objectives.

## Core Loop
1. Leaders issue military and logistical [[Task]]s.
2. Squads execute [[Plan]]s with role-specific [[Abilities]].
3. Battles, scouting, and control changes are resolved as [[Event]] chains.
4. Reputation, supply lines, and front lines are updated for the next cycle.

## Simulae Fit
- Uses [[Task]], [[Plan]], [[Event]], and faction-level social relationships.
