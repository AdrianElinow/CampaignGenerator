[[Assassin]] is a Yogscast-used role in [[Trouble in Terrorist Town]].

- Team Classification: [[Yogscast Independent Roles|Independent]], [[Yogscast Traitor Roles|Traitor]]

## Role Function
Deals high damage to a designated target and reduced damage to others, cycling targets as players die.
