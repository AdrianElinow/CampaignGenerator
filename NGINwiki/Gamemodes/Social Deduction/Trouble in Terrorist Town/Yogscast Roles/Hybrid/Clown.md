[[Clown]] is a Yogscast-used role in [[Trouble in Terrorist Town]].

- Team Classification: [[Yogscast Jester Roles|Jester]], [[Yogscast Independent Roles|Independent]]
- Category Source: Yogscast TTT role taxonomy
- Vault Category: [[Yogscast Hybrid Roles|Hybrid]]

## Role Function
Starts as a jester-style non-lethal role and later awakens as a lethal evil clown in endgame states.
