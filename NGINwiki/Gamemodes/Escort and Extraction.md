[[Escort and Extraction]] is a [[Gamemode]] where one side must move protected targets through hostile territory.

## Core Loop
1. Command assigns route, decoy, and defense [[Task]]s.
2. Escort units coordinate abilities around mobility and threat interception.
3. Ambushes and breakthroughs resolve as chained [[Event]] outcomes.
4. Mission success depends on target survival and destination criteria.

## Simulae Fit
- Supports multi-agent coordination and dynamic replanning under pressure.
