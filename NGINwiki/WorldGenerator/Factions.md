Factions are non-physical [[Simulae]] entities that represent the collective will of its population of [[SimulaeNode]] 

## Types of Factions

Here are the list of basic factions:

| Faction Type | Description                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Politic      | organization oriented around accumulating power and authority |
| Corporation  | organization oriented around accumulating profits             |
| Militant     | organization oriented around exacting violence                |
| Cult         | organization oriented around religious gospel                 |
- Pursuits
	- Power and Authority (Political / Militant)
	- Money / Resource(s) (Corporation, Mercenaries, treasure-hunters)
	- Higher purpose (Religion)
	- Information (Intelligence group)
	- Goal-State (Charity, etc.)
- Outlooks
	- Law-Abiding or Criminal


These basic types can be further expanded into a list of more recognizable entities
1. Government -- A formal state entity. A combination of a Politic with authority and a Militant with the ability to directly apply force.
2. Gang / Organized Crime family -- A 'corporation' (and perhaps 'government' in some aspects) in blatant disregard for the law.

3. Mercenary company -- A combination of 'Militant' and 'Corporation' wielding physical violence in pursuit of profit
